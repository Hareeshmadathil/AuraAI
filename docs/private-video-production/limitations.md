# Limitations

- Windows SAPI quality depends on locally installed voices and is not equivalent
  to a studio narration performance.
- Missing real evidence is represented by conspicuous internal placeholders,
  not fabricated screenshots.
- No footage, music, sound effects, fonts, models, or voices are downloaded.
- Rendering an eight-to-twelve-minute 1080p draft may be slow on a 4 GB i3
  system; the 720p preview is intended for earlier checks.
- The deterministic quality score does not predict views, revenue, retention,
  or commercial success.
- The local dashboard is unauthenticated and should remain bound to a trusted
  local interface.
- Publishing remains outside this workflow and must be a separate founder action.
