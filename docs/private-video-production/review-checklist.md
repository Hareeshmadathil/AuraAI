# Private draft review checklist

- Synthetic voice naturalness and pronunciation
- Pacing and hook impact
- Story clarity and evidence visibility
- Visual variety without distracting changes
- Subtitle readability and safe placement
- Audio clarity and licensed-music balance, if applicable
- AuraAI brand consistency
- Factual accuracy and privacy
- Placeholder count and required replacements
- Overall watchability

Allowed decisions are `approve_private_draft`, `request_edit`, and
`reject_private_draft`. None grants upload or publishing permission.
