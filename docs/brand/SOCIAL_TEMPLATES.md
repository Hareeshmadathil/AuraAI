# AuraAI Social and Presentation Templates

These are layout rules, not publishable assets or account instructions.

## Corporate announcement card

Use a compact concept mark, one clear heading, a short evidence-based statement,
and a footer identifying AuraAI. Keep clear space and avoid performance claims.

## Product-status card

Lead with the stage and visible status label. Show one artifact or metric only
when supplied by runtime data. Include “sample” or “local review” labels where
applicable.

## Presentation title slide

Use the full wordmark proposal, Deep Ink background, Aura Aqua accent, one
sentence of positioning, and founder attribution. Do not use channel branding
until a separate channel identity has been approved.

## Avatar and thumbnail

Use a compact app-icon concept centered within safe margins. Do not add robots,
brains, platform logos, copyrighted characters, fake analytics, or excessive
glow. Public use requires founder selection and legal screening first.
