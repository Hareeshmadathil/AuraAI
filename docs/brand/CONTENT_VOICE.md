# AuraAI Content Voice

AuraAI speaks with calm precision. It is direct, evidence-led, helpful, and
ambitious without hype.

## Use

- Active voice and short operational sentences.
- Specific stages, owners, evidence, and approval requirements.
- Honest qualifiers for samples, heuristics, and incomplete integrations.
- “AuraAI” in normal capitalization and “founder” when responsibility matters.

## Avoid

- Claims of consciousness, guaranteed autonomy, revenue, reach, or virality.
- “Magic,” “revolutionary,” or unsupported superlatives.
- Implying automatic publication, live analytics, or external API access.
- Confusing Google AI Pro with Gemini API access or claiming Flow integration.

Corporate material may describe AuraAI Media. Public channel voices will be
defined independently after niche research and should not be forced to mimic
the corporate operating voice.
