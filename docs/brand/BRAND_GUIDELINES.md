# AuraAI Brand Guidelines

## Naming

Use **AuraAI** in normal copy. **AuraAI Media** is an optional descriptive
business label. Do not use “Aura AI,” “Aura Ai,” “Aura-AI,” or all-capital
“AURAAI” as the default name. Python packages and public APIs keep their
existing names.

## Identity hierarchy

1. AuraAI — internal platform and parent-company identity.
2. AuraAI Media — optional label for media operations.
3. Flagship Channel Brand — a future independent public identity, not chosen.

## Application

Use the full wordmark where context is limited, the compact mark in established
AuraAI product chrome, and icon-only treatment only when an accessible label is
present. Maintain contrast on dark and light fields and prefer monochrome when
color reproduction is uncertain.

Do not stretch, rotate, recolor arbitrarily, add glow effects, place on noisy
imagery, combine concepts, remove clear space, or present a concept as approved.
Future departments extend tokens rather than introducing isolated colors.

## Recognition and inheritance

AuraAI should remain recognizable through naming, calm geometry, tokenized
color, typography, and operating language across products and presentations.
Channel brands inherit quality and accessibility principles, not necessarily
the corporate logo or palette.
